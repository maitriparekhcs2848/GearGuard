# GearGuard

GearGuard is a maintenance tracking application designed to manage equipment, maintenance teams, and maintenance requests in a simple and structured manner.

---

## Overview

The system helps organizations track their assets and handle both corrective and preventive maintenance through a clear workflow. It connects equipment with responsible teams and ensures maintenance tasks are properly scheduled, assigned, and completed.

---

## Features

- Equipment management with ownership and location tracking  
- Maintenance team and technician assignment  
- Corrective and preventive maintenance requests  
- Workflow-based request lifecycle (New → In Progress → Repaired → Scrap)  
- Kanban-style task tracking  
- Calendar-based scheduling for preventive maintenance
